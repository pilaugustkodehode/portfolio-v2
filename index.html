ABOUT

Yello! My name is Pil August Ekanger, a 26 year old queer and non binary website developer focusing on design. I’m proficient in HTML, CSS and Figma, and would say I'm intermediate in JavaScript. I'm based in Norway, and fluent in both Norwegian and English.

Previously, I’ve worked with a bit of design through my bachelors degree in theatre. This included making posters, or for the bachelor itself; concept designs for scenography options. I’ve also been designing stream overlays for myself. Within coding, I’ve only recently started making websites from scratch, but have previously used carrd.co to design some portfolios. I find it very exciting to do it from scratch with HTML and CSS, and it’s something I’ve picked up rather fast.

I’ve always been fond of technology, and grew up entranced with it. To this day, most of my free time is spent either drawing digitally, playing video games and the likes. At the moment, my current obsession is Baldur’s Gate 3, which I play both on PC and Playstation 5. There’s truly nothing like kicking back on the couch and slaying your way through Faerûn after a stressful day. I also spend some time on social media under my personas name, where I post whatever I’ve drawn the last few days. Currently I’m doing a December calendar where I draw different Pokémon, which has been a huge interest of mine since I was a wee lad.

I absolutely love working with design, and it is something I have an eye for. The dream would be to work with designing and art, which would let me combine my two biggest interests; art and technology. I have a burning need to leave something behind, to let my presence have been more than a shadow in the dark when I’m gone, and I feel like contributing to making things that stir people’s emotions would be a wonderful way to do this. Therefore, I’m excited about what the future will hold, and I’m motivated to grow as a person and designer as I go.
